President Laneice Jones

Greetings Sorors and Friends! The Detroit Alumnae Chapter of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., home of more than 725 members, is pleased to welcome you to our official website. Chartered more than 76 years ago, the Detroit Deltas are very excited about the work we do in the metropolitan Detroit community to carry out the vision of the 22 illustrious Founders of our sisterhood.
